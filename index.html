Black Beauty

"live your eyeliner,breathe your lipstick ,and kill for each other."

I made this poster keeping in mind the beauty you find, whether it is dark or light and white or black. You will find beauty everywhere. People fail to identify real beauty due to discrimination, but they don’t realize that once beauty lies inside once soul

journey

"The only journey is the one within."

Just after birth, our journey starts to the edge. Some of us manage to figure out the path and reach the edge of success while some don’t. But even we see all the steps of our journey it is just a small step. This made me think to make a poster with something like this.

WBR

"Black and white create a strange dreamscape that colors never can."

WBR stands for white black red. In history, people fight with colors such as black skin and white skin which result in the bloody war. I made this poster to show that colors is just the physical appearance. The personality of once is hidden inside the soul.
